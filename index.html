About

Since 1987, the Fourie family has built AgriuSec into the Free State's most trusted agricultural partner. What started as 400 hectares of farmland has grown into a 1,200-hectare operation serving the entire region. From our humble beginnings growing maize and sunflowers, we've expanded to offer everything from premium livestock to fresh meat processing and equipment sales. Three generations of farming wisdom combined with modern sustainable practices make AgriuSec your one-stop solution for agricultural excellence.
